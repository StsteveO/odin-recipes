Your Way Nachos

Something this easy to make should not taste this good.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Sauce 1/2 of your choice (Guac)
  • Sauce 2/2 of your choice (Sour cream)
  • Cheese 1/2 of your choice (Whatever cheese you pick, make sure it's shredded)
  • Cheese 2/2 of your choice (Again, make sure it's shredded)
  • Protein of your choice (Minced ground beef)
  • (Optional) veggie of your choice (shredded lettuce)
  • (Optional) Spice of your choice (Personally I am not a spice person but most people use jalapeno)

Remember: you want to make your nachos close to when you plan on eating, this is not something you make for later. Once you make your nachos, the tortilla chips can only remain chrunchy for so long before they eventually turn soggy. Nobody likes soggy nachos.

Directions

  1. Prepare all your ingredients. This includes making your your ground beef is cooked and ready to be spread.
  2. On a clean plate pour out your torilla chips to create a base; not all of them, just what you plan on eating.
  3. Next you want to layer both cheeses over the tortilla chips.
  4. Layer your protein over the cheese.
  5. Layer more cheese over the protein.
  6. Layer your veggie choice, and your spice choice over your cheese.
  7. Place in a microwave, and cook until the cheese has melted
  8. Remove from microwave and...
  9. Enjoy!
