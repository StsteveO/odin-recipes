Easy Hoagie

When you are starving, and need something you CAN'T mess up.

Ingredients

  • Hoagie roll of your choice
  • Condiment of your choice (best choice is Mayo!)
  • Oil of your choice (Extra virgin olive oil is best)
  • (optional) butter
  • Protein of your choice (roast beef for the win)
  • Veggie 1/3 of your choice (lettuce anyone?)
  • Veggie 2/3 of your choice (tomato)
  • Veggie 3/3 of your choice (onion)
  • Seasoning(s) of your choice (good 'ol oregano)

Directions

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Sprinkle garlic powder (optional) on the buttered side.
  2. With buttered side down place 1-2 slices of cheese on "un-buttered" side.
  3. Spray olive oil in skillet pan over medium heat.
  4. Place both slices of bread in skillet pan, buttered side down.
  5. Cook until cheese is starting to melt, and bread is golden.
  6. Flip one piece of bread on top of the other, and continue cooking until the cheese on both slices are melted together.
  7. Take out of skillet pan, and let cool.
  8. Enjoy!
